About Potato-chapChap


Introduction:
Jungle Investors is a start-up by young people from Thika Town Constituency whose dream is to create jobs and wealth for themselves and fellow Kenyan youths. The start-up is currently engaging in potato value addition and is at the market penetration stage. Initially, the operations will be centralized within Thika and its environs, but will be scaled-up to the rest of Kenya.

We deliver fresh potatoes free from any preservative chemicals. We don't use any chemicals for preservations.
